FAQs


What flavours do you have?

Vanilla, Chocolate, Red Velvet, Coffee, Carrot, orange, lemon.



Do you have a vegetarian (eggless) cake option?

Yes, we do make vegetarian cakes upon request.



How do you price your cakes?

Depending on the amount of people it will feed and the design. Averages at $3.50 per slice.



How far in advance do I need to place an order?

Custom cake orders that are on the simpler end of the spectrum require 1-2 weeks.


How do you place orders?

Call or Text (604)-240-1471 or Email shyamagalabadage@gmail.com
Include:
-Day of event
-Desired amount of servings
-Design or theme
-Any picture inspiration you may have



Do you provide samples?

No, individual cakes can be purchased for tasting.


Do you deliver?

Yes, upon request.



Do you do edible prints on cakes?

Yes, we do provide edible printing services.


Do you do wedding/party favors?

Yes, we do. We have a variety of options to choose from, from cookies to cupcakes.
